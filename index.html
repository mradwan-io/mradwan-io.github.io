About

Brief Introduction

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronics and Electrical Communications at Cairo University's Faculty of Engineering, where I joined in July 2025 after completing my PhD at The Ohio State University.

My doctoral research at OSU (2020–2024) focused on novel Decision Feedback Equalizer (DFE) structures for wireline and optical receivers — reconfigurable multi-stage PAM-N DFEs with linear hardware complexity for energy-proportional data transfer.

Before academia, I spent 3+ years designing production power management IPs at Vidatronic Inc. and interned at Intel Labs in 2022, working on a scalable high-PSR capacitor-less LDO.
